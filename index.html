Our Mission, Beyond Service

Welcome to the Eagles Nest Veteran Equine Therapy website. We are dedicated to helping veterans through the healing power of equine therapy.

Veterans Equine Therapy Ranch

Welcome to the Veterans Equine Therapy Ranch, where we provide a unique and healing experience for our brave veterans. Our ranch is dedicated to helping veterans overcome physical and emotional challenges through the power of equine therapy.

Equine therapy has been proven to be an effective treatment for veterans dealing with PTSD, anxiety, depression, and other mental health issues. Our experienced team of therapists and trained horses work together to create a safe and supportive environment for our veterans to heal and thrive.

At the Veterans Equine Therapy Ranch, we offer a variety of programs and services tailored to meet the individual needs of each veteran. From therapeutic riding sessions to equine-assisted counseling, we are committed to helping our veterans regain their strength, confidence, and sense of purpose.

If you or a loved one is a veteran in need of support, we encourage you to explore our website and learn more about the transformative power of equine therapy. Contact us today to schedule a visit to our ranch and start your journey towards healing and recovery.

Our Services

At Eagles Nest Veteran Equine Therapy, we offer a range of services to support our veterans in their healing journey:

  • Therapeutic Riding Sessions
  • Equine-Assisted Counseling
  • Equine-Assisted Learning Programs
  • Equine-Assisted Activities
  • Equine-Assisted Workshops

Each of our services is designed to provide a unique and impactful experience, tailored to the specific needs of our veterans. Our team of experienced therapists and trained horses work together to create a safe and supportive environment for our veterans to explore, heal, and grow.

Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can support you or your loved one on the path to healing and recovery.
